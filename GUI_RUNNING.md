## 🎯 **Key Features Available:**

✅ **Video Upload**: Drag & drop or click to upload  
✅ **Multiple Formats**: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, FLV, WMV  
✅ **Transcription**: WhisperX with 5 model sizes
